01

How We Deliver

VoltzHub is a fully digital store. All products — including software keys, game keys, game credits, and subscription credentials — are delivered digitally via WhatsApp.

There is no physical shipping, no packaging, and no waiting for a courier. Once your payment is confirmed, we send your product directly to your WhatsApp number.

⚡ Instant Delivery is our standard. Most orders arrive within 1–15 minutes of payment confirmation.
02

Delivery Timeframes

Our typical delivery timeframes are:

  • Software Keys & Game Keys: 1–10 minutes after payment confirmation.
  • Game Credits (UC, Diamonds, Robux, COD Points): 5–20 minutes — requires your Player ID or username.
  • Subscription Access (Netflix, Spotify, YouTube, etc.): 1–15 minutes after confirmation.
  • AI Tool Access (ChatGPT Plus): 5–20 minutes.

During peak hours or high order volume, delivery may take up to 30 minutes. If you haven't received your product after 30 minutes, please contact our support team immediately.

Our support team is available 24/7 to ensure your order reaches you without delay.
03

What You Need to Provide

For certain products, we require additional information from you to complete delivery:

  • Game Credits (PUBG UC, Free Fire Diamonds, Roblox Robux, COD Points): Your in-game Player ID and server region.
  • Subscription products: Just your WhatsApp number — no other details needed.
  • Software & Game Keys: No additional info needed — key is sent directly.
  • ChatGPT Plus: Your existing OpenAI account email address.
Please double-check your Player ID before sharing it with us. We cannot reverse credits sent to an incorrect account.
04

Delivery Confirmation

Once your product is delivered, you will receive a WhatsApp message containing your key, credentials, or confirmation of top-up. Please check:

  • That the key is complete and not truncated.
  • That credentials include both username/email and password.
  • That game credits reflect in your account within the stated timeframe.

If anything looks incorrect or incomplete, contact us immediately. Do not attempt to activate a key you suspect is incorrect — contact us first.

05

Failed or Delayed Delivery

In the rare event your delivery is delayed or fails:

  • Contact us on WhatsApp at +92 331 4887713 — we monitor our queue constantly.
  • Provide your name and the product you ordered.
  • We will re-process your order or escalate it immediately.

Delays can occasionally occur due to high demand, but we never abandon an order. Every paid order is fulfilled or refunded.

We have a 100% fulfilment rate — if you paid, you will receive your product.
06

International Delivery

VoltzHub serves customers primarily in Pakistan, but many of our products are global keys compatible worldwide. We deliver to any WhatsApp number, regardless of location.

Please check the product listing for any region restrictions before purchasing. Products labelled 'Global Key' are compatible in all regions.

If you're outside Pakistan and unsure about compatibility, message us before ordering — we'll confirm instantly.
